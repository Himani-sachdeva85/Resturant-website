OUR JOURNEY

The Skylight Garden

Founded in 2016, The Skylight Garden was created with a vision to offer an extraordinary rooftop dining experience where exceptional cuisine, elegant ambience, and heartfelt hospitality come together beneath the stars.

Every detail has been thoughtfully designed—from handcrafted signature dishes to breathtaking rooftop views—creating moments that transform every meal into a cherished memory.
